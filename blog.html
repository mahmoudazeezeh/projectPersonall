About Arraba

Arraba is a historical Palestinian town located in the northern West Bank, near Jenin. Known for its olive groves and traditional heritage, Arraba is home to ancient architecture and a close-knit community that values its cultural roots.

For more details, visit the Wikipedia page about Arraba.

Mahmoud Darwish: The Poet of Palestine

Mahmoud Darwish was one of Palestine's most beloved poets, celebrated worldwide for his poignant poetry about love, exile, and identity. His works continue to resonate with audiences globally, as they capture the spirit of the Palestinian people and the longing for freedom.

Learn more about his life and works on Wikipedia.
